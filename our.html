OUR STORY

Every great journey begins with love—and ours began with a wagging tail.


Like many dog parents, we know the joy of seeing bright eyes waiting by the door, the comfort of a warm cuddle after a long day, and the unspoken promise that our furry friends give us: unconditional love. But along with that love comes responsibility—the responsibility to keep them safe, happy, and cared for.


Our story began when we realised how much trust our dogs place in us every single day. They can’t tell us when they’re uncomfortable, they can’t ask for safety when they’re scared, and they can’t speak their names if they’re ever lost. That moment hit us deeply. We knew we had to create products that not only make dogs look good, but also keep them safe and comfortable.


So we set out to design personalised collars that reflect the bond between people and their pets—crafted with care, built to last, and designed with safety at the heart. By engraving your dog’s name and number, every collar we make carries the same promise: your dog’s safety and wellbeing always comes first.


What started as a small idea to protect one beloved pup has now grown into a mission to help thousands of pet parents feel at ease—knowing their best friend is safe, stylish, and happy.


Because at the end of the day, this brand isn’t just about dog collars. It’s about love, safety, and the unbreakable bond we share with our four-legged family.



With love,
Catherina and Raol
Founders, Calming Doggo
